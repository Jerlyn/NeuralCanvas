About Me

Hello! I'm Jerlyn, an Experience Design Director from the Commonwealth of Dominica with a passion for creating at the intersection of technology and creativity.

As a professional member of the iAAP and an Adobe Community Expert, I'm deeply committed to accessible design that works for everyone. When I'm not coding or designing, you might find me running ultramarathons or working on my next book or illustration.

This portfolio showcases my experiments with generative AI, interactive experiences, and creative coding. I believe in the power of STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Art, and Math) to solve problems and create meaningful experiences.
